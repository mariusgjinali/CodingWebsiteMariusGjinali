Health and Wellbeing doesn't just include doing lots of exercise. Primarily, Health and Wellbeing focuses on ensuring a healthy body, through things like excercise, diet and even cleanliness!

Starting with a healthy diet, it's important to know that you need to eat a little bit of everything, even veggies! This is what is known as a balanced diet, which is essential to a healthy life!

It's equally important though, to stay healthy by excercising! You don't need a lot of excercise, but it's important to run around and burn some calories a few times a day!


Another part of Health and Wellbeing that a lot of people ignore, as mentioned before, is cleanliness!

Cleanliness is super important, being clean lets you evade things like disease, which especially in this day and age, is extremely important! After all, it wouldn't do for you to get sick, now would it?

How can you maintain cleanliness though, it must be hard? Not at all! It's super easy, just remember to do things like washing your ands after you eat and go to the washroom, and not only will you stay healthy, but you'll help everyone around you stay healthy as well!

What can you do to help?


So what can you do to achieve better Health and Wellbeing? Well, by following the steps above, you can easily take yourself on a journey to becoming healthier! Still though, how can you make sure EVERYONE is staying nice and healthy? Well, it's not easy, but you can try and learn more about the issue and spread the word!
