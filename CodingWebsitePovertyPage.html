So we now know that poverty is a very dangerous issue, but just how dangerous is it? And what can we do to help?

Poverty is extremely dangerous, as lower quality of life changes can result in the creation of more dangerous neighborhoods and the like, which not only means an even lower quality of life for the residents of those neighborhoods, it also decreases the liklelihood of someone born into poverty to get out of poverty.

That phenomenon, where someone born into poverty can't get out of it, is something called the poverty cycle. If someone's born into poverty, it generally means lower quality of education, and thus a, on average, lower paying job, and then finally resulting in a re-introduction into poverty. That being said, what can we really do about it? Odds are, you, dear reader, are very young, probably under 15. As a result, the best thing that you can currently do to adress poverty.... is learn about it.
