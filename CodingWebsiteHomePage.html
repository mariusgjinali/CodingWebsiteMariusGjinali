Poverty is not something that's very easy to measure. As a result, most countries use something called a poverty line, to measure whether or not someone is considered in poverty, based on the country. In fact, in Canada, about 6.4% of Canadians, more than 2 million people, live below the poverty line...

Being below the poverty line negatively affects your life in all sorts of ways. Of course, it results in a lower quality of life, but it also results in a reported decrease in happiness and satisfaction in life.

Poverty is also correlated to causing things like malnutrition, food insecurity, homelessness, lack of access to childcare and healthcare, along with all sorts of terrible things. That's why we as a community have to work together to make the world a better place! For everyone living in it...


Health and Wellbeing is extremely important. After all, It basically governs our day to day lives! It's what lets us go out and play baseball, or have a fun time at school with some friends.

However, a lot of people don't get to enjoy the same wonderful health and wellbeing that many of us do... In fact, about 1 in 3 people have malnutrition across the world!
